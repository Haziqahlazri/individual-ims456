Family

"The love of a family is a life's greatest blessing."

This is my beloved family. My family consists of my father, my mother, one older sister, one older brother, one younger brother, one younger sister and myself. My father's name is Wan Lazri Bin Wan Ismail and my mother's name is Rosmini Binti Mahmood. My parents are the pillars of my family and my father is the head of my family. He is the one whose decision relating to any matter is final and all of us do respect it. Right from my childhood, my family members have prepared me for the challenges that I’ll face in the years ahead. In addition to this, all of my family members help and serve each other at times of need. These qualities that I have learnt from my family has helped me to shape my adult life in a right manner.
